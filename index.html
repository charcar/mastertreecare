Services we provide



Small business means that Tom is with you, every step of the way. From quote to clean up, Tom will ensure that the job is done efficiently, safely, and beautifully. From a knee-high Japanese Maple to a towering fir, no job is outside of our scope.

We service the greater Portland area including Tigard, Beaverton, Gresham, and Lake Oswego.

Here are some things we can help you with:


  • Pruning, (windsail, hazard, thinning, ornamental, remove deadwood, height reduction)
  • Removal (from that pesky dying arbor vitae to a fir requiring a crane)
  • Stump Grinding (removing surface roots and grinding to below grade)
  • Cabling/Bracing
  • 24hr Emergency Service

We're Angie's Certified and received a Super Service award in 2015.
